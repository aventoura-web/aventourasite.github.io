    7-Day Detailed Itinerary

    Day-by-Day Breakdown of Your Adventure

    01

    Jakarta - Kansai

    DEPARTURE

    On the designated day you are expected to gather at the Soekarno Hatta International Airport to travel together to Kansai, Osaka. Overnight on the plane.

    ✈️ Overnight flight
    02

    Kansai - Osaka - Kyoto - Gifu

    EXPLORATION

    You are expected to arrive at Itami Airport in the morning, then head to the city of Osaka and photo stop at Osaka Castle. You will enjoy the experience of riding the super fast bullet train Shinkansen (short ride) to the city of Kyoto. In this culturally rich and charming city, you will visit Kiyomizu Temple as a Buddhist temple located on a hill with beautiful views. In the afternoon towards Gifu, upon arrival you will check in at the hotel and rest.

    HOTEL: GIFU WASHINGTON / or equivalent
    🍽️ Lunch 🍽️ Dinner
    03

    Gifu - Takayama - Toyama

    HERITAGE SITES

    After enjoying breakfast at the hotel, you will head to Takayama. Upon arrival, you will visit Takayama Old Town to walk Sannomachi Street with a quiet town atmosphere and many shops selling souvenirs and Sake Shop. Next you will visit Shirakawa Go, an area of traditional residential houses where the roofs are made of straw with a triangular shape like an open standing book, also designated as one of UNESCO World Heritage cultural areas. Stay overnight in Toyama.

    HOTEL: TOYAMA COMFORT / or equivalent
    🍽️ Breakfast 🍽️ Lunch 🍽️ Dinner
    04

    Toyama - Alpine Route - Matsumoto

    ALPINE ADVENTURE

    Your tour today is heading to Tateyama Tunnel by passing underground / underground and enjoying the stunning scenery at an altitude of 2,316m above sea level and you can see the beauty of Kurobe which includes Kurobe Dam, where you can take photos with amazing views with a background of Kurobe Lake and Tateyama Mountains, as well as the Monument for The Martyr to Duty to commemorate 171 workers who died in the construction of the Kurobe Dam. You will also cross the SNOW CORRIDOR with stunning views along the road, weather permitting. Don't forget you will be invited to ride an electric bus crossing Kanden Tunnel. Stay overnight in the Matsumoto area.

    HOTEL: MATSUMOTO RICHMOND / or equivalent
    NOTE: IF ALPINE ROUTE IS NOT AVAILABLE, IT WILL BE REPLACED WITH SHINHOTAKA ROPEWAY
    🍽️ Breakfast 🍽️ Lunch 🍽️ Dinner
    05

    Matsumoto - Kawaguchi

    FLOWERS & SHOPPING

    Your journey today to see the Shibazakura Festival, which is a flower festival where about 800,000 types of flowers bloom with bright colors accompanied by Mount Fuji as a background and there is a bazaar selling various Japanese specialties. Free time for you to shop at Gotemba Premium Outlet which sells various world-class items such as Boss, Armani, Nike, Adidas, Lacoste, and others at affordable prices. Stay overnight in Kawaguchi.

    Note: Rooms in the Kawaguchi area can be Western style / traditional Tatami style. The blooming period of flowers can be different each year, depending on natural and weather conditions.

    HOTEL: KAWAGUCHI FUJI JIRAGON / or equivalent
    🍽️ Breakfast 🍽️ Dinner
    06

    Kawaguchi - Tokyo

    GARDENS & CITY

    Your tour today visits Ashikaga Flower Park to witness beautiful flower displays. Then head to the capital Tokyo, you will walk through Shinjuku as one of the busiest stations and busiest corners at night. After that you head to the hotel to check in and rest. Stay overnight in Tokyo.

    The blooming period of flowers can be different each year, depending on natural and weather conditions.

    HOTEL: TOKYO TMARK CITY OMORI / or equivalent
    🍽️ Breakfast 🍽️ Lunch
    07

    Tokyo - Narita - Jakarta

    RETURN HOME

    Today you fly back to Jakarta. Thus your journey with PANORAMA JTB has ended. Thank you for your participation and togetherness, see you in our other outings.

    NOTE: CHILD NO BED ONLY FOR CHILDREN AGE 5 YEARS AND UNDER

    5-Day Detailed Itinerary

    Day-by-Day Breakdown of Your Adventure

    01

    Jakarta - Bangkok - Pattaya

    DEPARTURE

    On the designated day, gather at Soekarno Hatta International Airport for your flight to Bangkok. Upon arrival at Suvarnabhumi International Airport, complete immigration and baggage claim, then head directly to Pattaya. Upon arrival in Pattaya, visit Errawade Herbal Store and the Pattaya Floating Market — a four-region cultural market where you can buy Thai souvenirs and local specialties. In the afternoon, head to Terminal 21 Vintage Pattaya Mall for shopping and dinner. Transfer to hotel for rest.

    HOTEL: KASTEL PATTAYA / or equivalent
    🍽️ Dinner
    02

    Pattaya Tour

    EXPLORATION

    After breakfast at the hotel, head to Pattaya Beach for a morning stroll. Visit Nongnooch Village — a vast tropical garden with stunning Thai-style architecture and the chance to witness an impressive elephant and cultural show. After lunch, visit the Gem Gallery (including a Dark Train ride) and Thai Dried Food Store for local snacks and treats. In the afternoon, head to King Power Duty Free Mall for branded shopping. In the evening after dinner, explore Jodd Fair Night Market — one of Pattaya's most vibrant and popular night markets. Return to hotel.

    HOTEL: PANNARAI PRATUNAM HOTEL / or equivalent
    🍽️ Breakfast🍽️ Lunch🍽️ Dinner
    03

    Pattaya - Bangkok

    RIVER & TEMPLES

    After breakfast, begin a full-day tour of Bangkok. Start by cruising along the Chao Phraya River — Thailand's main river stretching approximately 372 km — and stop at the magnificent Wat Arun Temple (Temple of Dawn), renowned for its beautiful riverside architecture. After lunch, visit the Bangkok Honey Bee Store and continue to Asiatique River Night Market, one of Bangkok's largest and most vibrant night markets along the riverfront. Enjoy dinner before returning to the hotel.

    HOTEL: PANNARAI PRATUNAM HOTEL / or equivalent
    🍽️ Breakfast🍽️ Lunch🍽️ Dinner
    04

    Bangkok - Shopping & Dinner Cruise

    SHOPPING & ENTERTAINMENT

    After breakfast, enjoy free time for shopping at Platinum Fashion Mall and the Pratunam Shopping Area — a paradise for fashion and local goods at affordable prices. In the evening, visit The Icon Mall of Siam and enjoy the spectacular Water Fountain Show starting at 18:00. The highlight of the evening is a Special Dinner Cruise aboard the Chaophraya Princess, sailing the Chao Phraya River with stunning illuminated views of Bangkok's temples and skyline while enjoying Thai cuisine and live entertainment. Return to hotel.

    HOTEL: PANNARAI PRATUNAM HOTEL / or equivalent
    🍽️ Breakfast🍽️ Special Dinner Cruise
    05

    Bangkok - Jakarta

    RETURN HOME

    After breakfast at the hotel, you will be transferred to MBK Mall for a final shopping session before being taken to the airport for your flight back to Jakarta. In the afternoon, you will arrive at Soekarno Hatta International Airport. Thus your journey with Aventoura has come to a wonderful end — thank you for joining us, and see you on our next adventure!

    NOTE: CHILD NO BED ONLY FOR CHILDREN AGE 5 YEARS AND UNDER
